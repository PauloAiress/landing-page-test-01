top 3 jobs

1. Anesthesiologists: $271,440 The BLS defines anesthesiologists as physicians who “administer anesthetics and analgesics for pain management prior to, during, or after surgery.” This highly specialized career has topped the list of highest-earning professions.

2. Surgeons: $251,650 Although becoming a surgeon requires several years of specialized training, these physicians are rewarded with one of the highest-paying careers.6 Surgeons may find themselves working long, irregular hours, depending on their specialty.

3. Obstetricians-Gynecologists: $239,120 Doctors specializing in vaginal, ovarian, uterine, and cervical reproductive health and childbirth, known as obstetricians-gynecologists, or OB-GYNs, make slightly more than the annual wages listed for orthodontists
